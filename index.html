About us

JindrichAllison Oil & Gas Company is a London, UK based, independent energy company whose business is to maximize production from its shallow offshore North Sea properties and onshore properties in London, and Northern Ireland, and to use that cash flow to explore, develop, exploit, produce and acquire crude oil and natural gas properties across the United Kingdom.

Our mission is to create value for our shareholders through the acquisition, exploitation, exploration and development of oil and gas properties and by investing in a balanced portfolio of longer life reserves and high impact opportunities.
